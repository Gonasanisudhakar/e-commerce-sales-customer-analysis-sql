## &#x20;**E-Commerce Sales \& Customer Analysis – Key Insights**



##### &#x20;**💰 Revenue Insights**



* &#x20;A small number of product categories generate the majority of total revenue.
* &#x20;High-priced products contribute more revenue even with fewer orders.





##### &#x20;**🛒 Orders Insights**



* &#x20;Some products have very high order volume but low revenue.
* &#x20;This indicates they are low-priced and high-demand products.







##### &#x20;**👥 Customer Insights**



* &#x20;Most customers place only one order (low customer retention).
* &#x20;A small group of customers contributes a large share of total revenue.
* &#x20;These high-value customers are important for business growth.





##### **📈 Sales Trend Insights**



* &#x20;Revenue varies across different months.
* &#x20;Certain months show higher sales, indicating seasonal trends.





##### &#x20;**🎯 Business Insights**



* &#x20;Focus on high-revenue product categories to increase profit.
* &#x20;Improve customer retention strategies to increase repeat purchases.
* &#x20;Target high-value customers with offers and loyalty programs.







##### &#x20;**📌 Final Understanding**



* &#x20;Revenue is concentrated in a few categories and customers.
* &#x20;Customer behavior shows low retention but strong high-value segments.
* &#x20;Data analysis helps in making better business decisions.
